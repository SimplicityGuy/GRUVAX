<objective>
Close INT-A: the three `boundary_changed` publishes in `segments.py` (cut, override, insert-cut)
use the wrong payload field names (`cubes`/`unit_id` + a redundant top-level `type`) instead of
the canonical `cube_ids`/`unit` shape that every other producer (`cubes.py`, `import_.py`) and the
kiosk consumer (`KioskView.tsx`) use. On a segment edit the kiosk destructures `cube_ids=undefined`
and `for (const c of cube_ids)` throws a `TypeError`, so highlight-follows-record (D-05/D-06) and
shimmer-clear silently fail. This plan fixes all three publishes at source and hardens the kiosk
SSE handlers with try/catch (IN-02) so future payload drift degrades gracefully.

Purpose: Restore the "admin cut-point/override/insert-cut edit → live kiosk re-render" flow that
the v1.0 milestone audit flagged as a BLOCKER. (RTM-01, ADMN-11, SEG-07, SEG-08)
Output: Corrected SSE payload shape (backend), defensive SSE handlers (frontend), and three
regression tests that lock the payload contract so INT-A cannot silently recur.
</objective>
