<objective>
Close INT-B: `revert_change_set` in `history.py` reloads `BoundaryCache` after an undo but never
re-derives `SegmentCache` and never publishes `boundary_changed`. After an admin undo, `/api/locate`
returns stale segment positions until the next admin write or a restart, and the kiosk is never
notified (no live re-render). This plan wires `get_segment_cache` + `get_collection_snapshot` +
`get_event_bus` into `revert_change_set` and adds a post-commit re-derive + publish block that
mirrors the canonical pattern in `cubes.py:342-362`.

Purpose: Restore the "admin undo (history revert) → estimator update + live kiosk re-render" flow
that the v1.0 milestone audit flagged as a BLOCKER. (ADMN-09, RTM-01, ADMN-11, SEG-07)
Output: An undo that updates `/api/locate` immediately and live-re-renders the kiosk, plus two
regression tests proving the re-derive and the publish.
</objective>
